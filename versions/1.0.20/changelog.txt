Extension Craft 1.0.20

Welcome to town! The flagship town-building mod joins the pack.

- Renri's Town 1.1.0 (NEW) — Found a town with a town hall and grow it from a hamlet into a metropolis. Residents with names and personalities move into the houses you build (bed + door + roof), commute to their workplaces, tend the fields around the Harvest Box, and stroll to the park in the evening. Facilities (park / bathhouse / shop / clinic / guard post) are judged by tag-based conditions, so any design and any material works. Hiring a villager recruits them into town as a resident (villagers with trades are protected). Includes a town-boundary particle display, a warehouse GUI with search & tabs, and soft integration with Renri's Inn and the coin economy.

Extension Craft 1.0.20

ようこそ、街へ！旗艦の街づくりMODがパックに加わりました。

- Renri's Town 1.1.0（新規） — 役場を置いて街を設立し、集落から大都市へ育てる街づくりMOD。名前と性格を持つ住民が、あなたの建てた家（ベッド＋ドア＋屋根）に移住し、職場へ通勤し、収穫箱の周りの畑を世話し、夕方は公園へ寄り道します。施設（公園・浴場・商店・診療所・番所）はタグ条件で判定されるので、デザインも素材も自由。村人を雇うと街の住民として移住します（取引持ちの村人は保護）。街の境界パーティクル表示、検索・タブ付き倉庫GUI、Renri's Inn・コイン経済とのソフト連携つき。
