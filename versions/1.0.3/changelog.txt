# Extension Craft 1.0.3

- NEW: AmbientSounds — richer ambient soundscapes (forests, caves, and more)
- NEW: Drip Sounds — drip and drop sound effects for a moodier underground
- NEW: Simple Clouds — beautiful volumetric clouds
- PERFORMANCE: Added Sodium, Entity Culling, ImmediatelyFast, Dynamic FPS and MoreCulling — big client FPS boost
- The in-game guide book now has a new "Atmosphere & Ambience" section
