# Extension Craft 1.0.14

- UPDATE: Renri's Diner 1.1.3 — a big update to the diner.
  - Customers now arrive in a variety of outfits, show the dish they ordered as an icon above their head, and stay to eat for a while after being served instead of vanishing.
  - Decorating pays off: ambiance décor near the register raises how many customers can dine at once and adds tips (scan reaches up to 32 blocks).
  - 20 new decorations (39 total at the Decor Shop): maneki-neko, daruma, flower vases, kadomatsu, sake barrel, ramen & tempura samples, wall clock, a 2-block-tall vending machine, bottle crate, tea set, zabuton cushion (also a seat), rice cooker, trash bin, and wall-mounted paper menu & calendar.
  - Many decorations are now full 3D models; the wall clock, paper menu, and calendar mount on walls.
