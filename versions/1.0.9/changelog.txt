# Extension Craft 1.0.9

- NEW: FTB Chunks & FTB Teams — share one explored map with your team and see each other's positions on the map/minimap (chunk protection is OFF by default, so claims are map-only)
- NEW: Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks add-ons — Magic From The East, Twilight Spellbooks, Monsters & Spellbooks, and Crystal Chronicles (adds Fusion & Biolith)
- NEW: Cosmetic Armor Reworked — wear cosmetic armor over your real armor
- NEW: Simple Hats — collect and wear decorative hats
- CHANGE: Xaero's Minimap now defaults to the top-right corner and is locked to north
- UPDATE: Renri's Quest Post 1.0.2 — transceiver item requests now work with or without a posting station and across dimensions; if the requester is offline the items are delivered on their next login; up to 5 requests per player; you can now cancel your own request
